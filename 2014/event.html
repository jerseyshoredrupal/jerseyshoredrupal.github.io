Drupal 8 at the Jersey Shore

Saturday, July 12, 2014 at 9:00 AM to Sunday, July 13, 2014 at 5:00 PM
Asbury Park, NJ

Find Out More Register

AGENDA

Each day will begin at 9:45am with a quick organizing and discussion session followed by forming groups and working on issues. Our goal is to keep all attendees focused on contributing, so we will provide breakfast, lunch, and snacks throughout the day. On Saturday night we will go out on the town for dinner, followed by an evening of continued sprinting with beer and other refreshments.

SATURDAY, JULY 12

  • 9:00 - 9:45 = Check-in/Breakfast
  • 9:45 - 10:00 = Welcome
  • 10:00 - 12:45 = Sprinting
  • 12:45 - 1:30 = Lunch Available
  • 1:30 - 6:00 = Sprinting
  • 6:00 - 8:00 = Dinner (on your own)
  • 8:00 - 10:00 = Drinks & Sprinting

SUNDAY, JULY 13

  • 9:00 - 9:45 = Check-in/Breakfast
  • 9:00 - 12:30 = Sprinting
  • 12:30 - 1:15 = Lunch Available
  • 1:15 - 4:45 = Sprinting
  • 4:45 - 5:00 = Closing

Note that all attendees should have previously participated in a core sprint and/or have a fully working local environment for developing patches.

Driving

From North Jersey, NY area:

  • New Jersey Turnpike South to Exit 11 Garden State Parkway South.
  • Garden State Pkwy to exit 102 Asbury Ave Tinton Falls) bear right.
  • Merge onto Asbury Ave.
  • Continue onto NJ-35 S/Rte 35 S.
  • Turn left onto NJ-35 N/Rte 35 N.
  • Slight right onto Asbury Ave.
  • Turn right onto Main St.
  • Turn left onto Lake Ave.
  • Cowerks will be on the left.

From Philadelphia Area:

  • NJ Turnpike North to Exit 7A. Follow I-195 E approximately one half hour to Rte 18 N.
  • Take Rte 18 N a few miles to Exit 10A (Route 66 /Asbury Park).
  • Take Rte 66/Asbury Ave to the Asbury Circle.
  • Take the second exit on the right off of the circle to Asbury Ave (The Coca Cola building will be on your right).
  • Follow directions above from Asbury Avenue.

From South Jersey Area:

  • Garden State Parkway North to exit 100-A Asbury Park.
  • Follow Route 66/Asbury Avenue approximately 5 miles to the Asbury Circle.
  • Take the second exit on the right off of the circle to Asbury Ave (The Coca Cola building will be on your right).
  • Follow directions above from Asbury Avenue.

PARKING

You might be able to park for free on Bond St. between Main and Lake, however parking spaces are limited. Otherwise, parking on Lake Ave. in Asbury Park, NJ is enforced from 9am to 12am and costs $1.00 per hour. Several parking meters are located on the street or you can pay by Pay-By-Cell using the mPay2park app. Visit www.mPay2Park.com to set up an account and learn more. There is also a parking garage located on Bangs Ave.

Further information about parking: http://www.asburyparkchamber.com/parking.asp
